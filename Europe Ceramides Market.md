Definition of Ceramides Market
The Ceramides market refers to the ecosystem of products, services, and systems related to the production, formulation, and application of ceramides. Ceramides are a class of lipids found naturally in the skin and play a critical role in retaining moisture and protecting against environmental damage. The market includes synthetic, plant-based, and animal-derived ceramides, each catering to specific industry requirements. Key terms include "skin barrier function," "lipid bilayer," and "bioavailability," which are integral to understanding ceramides' role in skincare and beyond.
