The GIS software market refers to the ecosystem of tools, systems, and services designed to capture, store, analyze, and visualize geographical data. Key components include:

Services: Consulting, implementation, and training services that support GIS deployment and optimization.

Systems: Integrated solutions combining hardware, software, and data to deliver end-to-end GIS functionalities.
