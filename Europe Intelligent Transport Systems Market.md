Definition of Intelligent Transport Systems Market

The Intelligent Transport Systems market refers to a network of advanced systems and services designed to optimize the efficiency and safety of transportation networks. Key components include:

Products: Sensors, cameras, traffic control systems, and communication devices.

Services: Real-time data analytics, system integration, and consulting.

Systems: Traffic management, public transportation management, and smart parking solutions. These components work together to deliver smarter, safer, and more sustainable transportation solutions.

Commercial Vehicle Operations (CVO): Systems to streamline freight and logistics operations, such as electronic toll collection and fleet management.

Advanced Traveler Information Systems (ATIS): Solutions providing real-time updates on traffic, weather, and travel conditions.
