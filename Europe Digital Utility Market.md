Definition of Digital Utility Market

The Digital Utility market refers to the ecosystem of technologies, products, services, and systems that digitize and automate utility services. Key components include:

Products: Smart meters, sensors, and advanced grid infrastructure.

Services: Predictive maintenance, real-time monitoring, and data analytics.

Systems: Cloud-based platforms, IoT networks, and AI-driven decision support systems.

Key terms related to the market include:

Smart Grids: Electricity networks that use digital technology to monitor and manage energy flow.

Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI): Systems that enable two-way communication between utilities and customers.

Predictive Analytics: Tools that analyze data to forecast trends and optimize operations.
