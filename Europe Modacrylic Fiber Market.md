Definition of Modacrylic Fiber Market

The Modacrylic Fiber market is defined by the production, distribution, and application of synthetic fibers made from acrylonitrile and other monomers. Known for their flame-retardant properties and resilience, these fibers are a preferred choice for products requiring durability and safety.

Key components of the market include:

Products: Fibers, yarns, and woven/non-woven fabrics.

Services: Custom manufacturing and treatment processes.

Systems: Advanced spinning and dyeing techniques.
