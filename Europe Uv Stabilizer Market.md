Market Segmentation

By Type

Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS):

Provide long-term protection by scavenging free radicals formed during UV exposure.

Commonly used in polymers for outdoor applications.

UV Absorbers:

Absorb UV radiation and dissipate it as heat, preventing damage to materials.

Ideal for transparent applications like films and coatings.

Quenchers:

Neutralize high-energy states in molecules to prevent degradation.

Often used in combination with other stabilizers for enhanced effectiveness.

By Application

Construction:

Used in roofing materials, siding, and pipes to prevent UV-induced damage.
