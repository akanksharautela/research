DEFINITION OF LASER ENGRAVING MACHINE MARKET
The Laser Engraving Machine market refers to the ecosystem of machines, systems, and related services used for engraving materials using laser technology. This market includes various machine types, such as fiber lasers, CO2 lasers, and solid-state lasers, which operate by directing a high-intensity laser beam onto a material surface to create precise engravings or marks.

Key components of this market include:

Machines: Hardware used for laser engraving, ranging from compact desktop models to large industrial units.

Software: Tools enabling design and operation of laser engraving processes.

Services: Installation, maintenance, and customization services supporting machine users.

Terms such as beam intensity, wavelength, and raster engraving are central to understanding this market and its functionality.
